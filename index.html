Research

Exoskeleton Team @ Jadoo Technologies

Developing EEG-integrated helmet and robot suit for stroke rehabilitation. Self-teaching CAD, PCB design, and Makerpass training.

Memantine & Down Syndrome

Investigating memantine's efficacy as NMDA receptor antagonist to improve cognitive outcomes in Down Syndrome mice through SVM modeling.

ASDRP: Nanoparticle Research

Synthesized curcumin-coated iron-oxide nanoparticles using cilantro extract for Parkinson's disease. Won 1st place at UC Davis Teen Biotech Challenge.

Paper Diagnostics & Giant Vesicles

Researching paper diagnostic test kits, reagent calibration, and giant unilamellar vesicles (GUVs) for synthetic cell constructs.
